# Privacy Policy — Paralegal Toolkit (Bates Stamp)

**Last updated:** April 4, 2026

## Data collection

Paralegal Toolkit does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All PDF processing runs entirely on your local device.

## Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions, all used locally:

- **storage** — Saves your Bates stamp preferences (prefix, position, font, color) in your browser's local storage.
- **downloads** — Saves the stamped PDF file to your computer.
- **tabs** — Opens the PDF viewer in a new browser tab.
- **activeTab** — Detects if the current tab contains a PDF link for the right-click menu.
- **contextMenus** — Adds a right-click option to open PDF links in the Bates stamp viewer.

## Third-party services

This extension does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services. No network requests are made.
